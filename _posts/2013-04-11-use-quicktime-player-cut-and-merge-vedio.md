###Step 1
drag a vedio to QuickTime Player
###Step 2
hit shortcut: Cmd + T
###Step 3
drag the left and right yellow bar to cut vedio
###Step 4
File -> Export
###Step 5
when you need to merge. just drag another vedio to the vedio current playing,
and Export

That's all, so easy ,right?
